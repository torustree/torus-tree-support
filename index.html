Integration Support

For the Next Day or Two

You don't need to analyse everything right now. Let your body catch up. A few simple things can help.

Rest when you can
Drink water and eat something grounding
Avoid overstimulation where possible
Try a gentle walk, movement, or stretching
Use longer exhales if you feel activated
Put your feet on the floor and notice what's around you

What You Might Feel After Breathwork

Everyone's experience is different. Some people feel calm straight away. Others feel tender, emotional, tired, or more sensitive than usual. Vivid dreams can happen too.

This is normal after a deep session. Go gently — and please get proper medical support if anything feels unsafe or concerning.
